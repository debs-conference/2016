Registration

The deadline for early registration is June 1st, 2016. Register before this date to get discounted rates.

Note that we expect to receive at least one non-student conference registration for each paper, accepted in the research, industry, or grand challenge track by May 23. For each accepted poster or demo, there must be at least one student or non-student conference registration received by May 23. If the registration is not received in time, the paper will not be included in the proceedings.

If you are a student member of ACM/SIGMOD/SIGSOFT, but need a non-student conference registration because of a research, industray, or grand challenge paper, please select the option " Conference (incl. Workshops) Student Member of ACM/SIGMOD/SIGSOFT with paper that requires full rate" at the registration site. It is the same rate as for professional members of ACM/SIGMOD/SIGSOFT, but will accept a student membership number.

To register for the conference, please visit the RegOnLine registration site.

What is included in the registration? Registration includes participation in the main DEBS program as well as DEBS tutorials, the Doctoral Symposium, and the workshops (June 24). Registration also includes lunch every day, coffee breaks, a reception (June 21st), and the conference banquet (June 22). The workshop day only registration includes participation in the workshops (June 24) and includes lunch and coffee breaks for that day.

How to cancel the registration? Cancellation of conference registration will incur a $100 administrative charge that will be deducted from the amount refunded. You can cancel your registration through the registration site.

DEBS will be held back-to-back with SIGMOD'2016 @ San Francisco, CA (June 26 - July 1)
